About Us

Anveshak was founded in May 2008 as an engineering and IP service provider based in Chandler, AZ with a development center in Pune, India.

Over the years Anveshak has diversified in Information Technology(IT) services as well as developed web based products for the education and healthcare sectors.

Combining our expertise in mechanical engineering, software and hardware development,Anveshak is at the forefront in the field of Internet of Things with a focus on the energy and home automation sectors. Details on our offerings are in the product and serviceinformation pages.
